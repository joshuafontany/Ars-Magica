## Canonical Errata To Apply

- `Lesser Power` (p. 54): add the sentence explaining that the Virtue grants 25 spell levels and may be taken repeatedly.
- `An Exemplary Catalog of Faerie Powers` (p. 55): move the first paragraph of the third column into the `Improved Powers` Virtue description on p. 56.
- `Mormo` (p. 70): correct the wound-penalties line.
- `Increasing Sympathy Traits` (pp. 102-103): add the sentence explaining that a negative trait may be increased to 0 and then to 1.
- `Faerie Upbringing` (p. 115): classify it as a Personality Flaw rather than a General Flaw.
- `Faerie Doctors as Mythic Companions` (pp. 117-118): replace the outdated term with `Gender Nonconforming`.
- `Enchantment Rite` (p. 124): correct the worked-example arithmetic.
- `The Reflecting Pool` (p. 127): replace `Sympathy` with `Symbol` in both the RDT description and level calculation.

# Realms of Power: Magic Errata

Canonical errata source for the `Realms of Power: Magic` pilot.

## Source Status

- Official source: Atlas Games, Ars Magica Fifth Edition Errata
- URL: <https://www.atlas-games.com/arm5/arm5errata.php/>
- Local PDF matched: `Ars Magica 5e - Realms Of Power - Magic {AG0288}.pdf`

## Canonical Errata To Apply

- `Immobilize Prey` (p. 65): the Form changes from Mentem to Corpus, the level rises to 20, and the initiative figures are reduced.
- `Animal Ken` (p. 82): the text is revised so Animal Ken explicitly adds to taming totals.
- `Jinn` (p. 107): delete the suggested Forms, because later line development establishes that jinn can align with any Form.
